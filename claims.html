Claims FAQ

Answers to your most common questions

Most claims are processed and approved within 24-48 hours. We review your uploaded bills and confirm eligibility against your plan. Payment to your bank account usually happens within 2-3 business days after approval.

Yes! You can visit any licensed veterinary clinic in India. There are no network restrictions. You pay the vet directly, and we reimburse you to your bank account. This gives you complete freedom to choose your preferred veterinarian.

You'll need to provide:

  • Your vet's invoice or receipt
  • Medical records or prescription (if applicable)
  • Pet's health report or diagnosis document
  • Proof of payment (if paid separately)

Photos or scans are fine—we don't need originals.

Yes. Most plans have category-based limits. For example, accidents/illnesses/surgeries have annual limits of ₹25,000. Vaccinations cap at ₹5,000/year, and diagnostics at ₹10,000/year. Check your specific plan for exact limits. You can claim multiple times as long as you stay within annual limits.

If we can't approve your claim, we'll explain why clearly within 48 hours. Common reasons include:

  • Condition falls under exclusions
  • Waiting period hasn't passed yet
  • Amount exceeds annual limit
  • Pre-existing condition (in year 1)

We'll work with you to understand your options. Message us on WhatsApp for support.
