Our Story

Built by a Pet Parent, Not a Corporation

Prapanch created GuavaSure after watching helpless pet parents struggle with unexpected vet bills and claim denials. As a dog parent himself, he knew there had to be a better way — a membership that actually cares about your pet's health, not just profits.

GuavaSure isn't insurance company politics. It's a wellness membership built on the simple belief that every pet deserves access to quality care, without the financial fear. Backed by Go Digit General Insurance Limited, but designed by someone who truly understands the bond between you and your pet.
