Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

There's no age limit for coverage! We insure pets from 8 weeks old to senior ages (10+ years). Puppies get lower premiums, and we don't discriminate based on age. Just note that pre-existing conditions (before policy start) aren't covered.

You can claim after 30 days of your policy activation. Just upload your vet invoice via our app, and we'll approve & reimburse within 24 hours. For emergency surgeries, we process even faster—some claims approved in 4 hours.

Yes! Golden Retrievers prone to hip dysplasia, Bulldogs with breathing issues, Dachshunds with back problems—all covered. We don't exclude any breeds or genetic conditions (except pre-existing ones before policy activation).

Absolutely. Use any AAHA-registered vet in India. We have 5,000+ partner clinics, but you're not limited to them. Go to your trusted vet, we'll reimburse. For cashless claims, use our partner network for instant billing settlement.

Conditions existing before policy activation aren't covered in year 1. But here's the good news: if your pet is healthy through year 1 and the condition resolves, it may be covered from year 2 onward. Talk to our team about your specific case.

Pay via Razorpay (cards, UPI, netbanking) or direct bank transfer. Monthly plans auto-renew via your chosen method. EMI available on select credit cards (contact us for details). No extra charges for monthly plans—same premium either way.
