We are NOT an insurance company.

We're the friend who got a ₹15,000 bill for a "routine checkup."

person

That friend is me, Prapanch.

That "routine checkup" became a scan. Became a diagnosis. Became medication. Became a home visit. What I thought was ₹2,000 came to ₹15,000.

I'd spent years in New York in insurance tech. I watched companies abroad completely change what pet insurance means. Then I came back to India. The gap between what pet parents here deserve and what actually exists was impossible to ignore.

Who we built this for

The young couple in Bangalore. Entry-level jobs. A rented flat. A dog they love with all their heart.

Something goes wrong and now a procedure is ₹35,000. That's a month's rent.

They now have two choices: burn through savings or delay their pet's care.

Neither of those should exist.
