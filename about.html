How GuavaSure Started

Prapanch's best friend called one afternoon in panic. Her dog had a routine checkup at the vet. The bill? Rs. 15,000. She didn't have that kind of money lying around. The vet visit wasn't even an emergency—just preventive care.

That conversation sparked a realization: Most pet parents in India have zero coverage. There's no safety net when their furry family members get sick. The few insurance options that exist are expensive, riddled with clauses, and a nightmare to claim from.

So we built GuavaSure. Not as an insurance company trying to maximize claims rejections. But as a platform built by pet parents, for pet parents. We partnered with Go Digit General Insurance Limited—a rock-solid insurer—to underwrite the coverage. We handle the tech, the simplicity, and making sure your pet gets the care they deserve.

Because every pet parent deserves peace of mind.
