About Guavasure

We're a pet wellness platform — wellness comes first; protection is part of what we facilitate.

Built by pet parents who saw the gap

When a routine vet visit cost ₹15,000 and left our pet parents scrambling, we realized something was broken. Pet healthcare in India lacked structure, transparency, and affordable protection.

That's when Guavasure was born — not just as an insurance facilitator, but as a wellness-first platform where every pet parent has access to preventive care, digital health records, and trusted protection.

Today, we're building the pet wellness ecosystem India deserves.
