About GuavaSure

GuavaSure is a pet wellness membership and coverage platform. We partner with Go Digit Insurance to provide comprehensive pet health coverage.

Unlike traditional insurance, we make pet coverage simple, transparent, and stress-free. You can claim at any vet in India—no network restrictions.

Our plans cover accidents, illnesses, surgeries, vaccinations, diagnostics, and more. We process claims in 24-48 hours with direct bank transfers.

GuavaSure was founded by Prapanch, after a personal experience where his friend faced a ₹15,000+ vet bill without proper coverage.

That moment sparked the idea to create a pet wellness platform that's truly accessible, transparent, and pet-friendly for all Indian pet parents.

Coverage

Our comprehensive plans cover:

  • Accidents & injuries (fractures, burns, wounds)
  • Illnesses (infections, allergies, diseases)
  • Surgeries (including anesthesia and post-op care)
  • Tick fever & parasites
  • Vaccinations & booster shots
  • Diagnostic tests (X-rays, ultrasounds, blood work)

Each category has annual limits, ranging from ₹5,000 to ₹25,000 depending on your plan.

Standard exclusions include:

  • Cosmetic procedures (ear cropping, tail docking, etc.)
  • Pre-existing conditions (for the first year)
  • Breeding-related expenses
  • Routine wellness (beyond what's listed as covered)
  • Pet grooming and boarding
  • Conditions from mating or fighting

We'll clarify any exclusions specific to your pet during enrollment.

No waiting period for accidents—you're covered immediately.

For illnesses and other conditions, there's a 30-day waiting period from enrollment to allow your pet's health to stabilize.

Annual limits by category:

  • Accidents, Illnesses, Surgeries: Up to ₹25,000/year
  • Tick Fever & Parasites: Up to ₹25,000/year
  • Vaccinations: Up to ₹5,000/year
  • Diagnostics: Up to ₹10,000/year

Once you hit the annual limit in a category, you can claim again in the next calendar year.

Claims

Filing a claim is simple:

  1. Pay your vet directly
  2. Take photos of your vet bills and medical records
  3. Share via WhatsApp, email, or our portal
  4. We review and approve within 24-48 hours
  5. Money goes to your bank account

Claims processing is fast:

  • Review & approval: 24-48 hours
  • Bank transfer: 2-3 business days after approval
  • Most claims approved without extra questions

We process claims on weekdays and holidays, so you get reimbursed quickly.

Currently, we offer reimbursement—you pay the vet, and we reimburse you to your bank account.

We're building a cashless network of partner vets where you can get zero upfront cost. This feature is coming soon!
