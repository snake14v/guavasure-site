About GuavaSure

What is GuavaSure? expand_more

GuavaSure is a wellness membership and technology platform that works alongside insurance coverage provided by Go Digit General Insurance Limited. We combine monthly wellness membership (starting at ₹249/month) with insurance coverage for accidents, illnesses, surgery, and more. Our digital health records platform lets you track your pet's health in one place and file claims easily through our app or WhatsApp.

Are you an insurance company? expand_more

No, GuavaSure is not an insurance company. We are a wellness membership and technology platform. Insurance coverage is provided by Go Digit General Insurance Limited. GuavaSure handles the technology, member experience, and claims support, while Go Digit is the insurer. This means the best of both worlds: our pet-parent-first technology and Go Digit's trusted insurance backing.

Why was GuavaSure started? expand_more

Our founder Prapanch's routine vet checkup turned into a ₹15,000 medical journey. It sparked a mission: why should pet parents in India face impossible choices between their pet's health and financial security? Prapanch spent years in insurance tech abroad, saw what works globally, and returned to India to build what was missing. GuavaSure exists because we believe every pet parent deserves simple, affordable, reliable coverage.

Plans & Pricing

How much does it cost? expand_more

GuavaSure wellness membership starts at ₹249 per month. This includes accidents, illnesses, surgery, vaccinations, digital health records, and more. No hidden fees. You can cancel anytime if you're not satisfied.

What's included? expand_more

At ₹249/month, you get: 1) Coverage for accidents and illnesses, 2) Annual vaccinations included, 3) ₹500 annual care benefit for wellness checks, 4) Digital health records (health ID), 5) Works at any vet in India, 6) Cashless support at partner clinics. Plus email and WhatsApp support whenever you need it.

Are there different plans? expand_more

Every dog (and cat, and bunny) is different. We're working on expanding plan options to fit different pets and budgets. For now, visit our Plans page to see what's available for your pet, or chat with us on WhatsApp about customizing coverage for your specific needs.

Can I cancel? expand_more

Yes, you can cancel anytime without penalty. No long-term contracts. However, we'd love to know why you're leaving. Check your plan terms for specific cancellation procedures, or reach out on WhatsApp and we can help guide you through the process.

Coverage

Which vets can I visit? expand_more

GuavaSure works at any licensed veterinary clinic in India. No vet network restrictions. Visit your trusted local vet, go to a specialty hospital, or try a new clinic—we've got you covered. Cashless support is available at select partner clinics. Reimbursement claims work everywhere.

What about pre-existing conditions? expand_more

Pre-existing conditions are handled according to your plan terms and Go Digit's underwriting. Coverage details depend on your pet's health history when you sign up. For specifics about your pet, check your policy document or chat with us on WhatsApp.

What's the waiting period? expand_more

Waiting periods are determined by Go Digit, our insurance partner. Standard industry practice applies. Your policy document will specify when coverage begins for different claim types. You'll see this clearly during signup.

Claims

How do I file a claim? expand_more

Simple: Visit your vet, get your pet treated, and collect the itemized receipt and prescription. Then upload the documents through the GuavaSure app or send them via WhatsApp. For cashless claims, verify your vet is a partner clinic during check-in.

Is cashless support available? expand_more

Yes, at select partner clinics across India. Confirm with your vet before the visit. If they're not a partner, file a reimbursement claim and we'll refund you after submission. Either way, you're covered.

How long does reimbursement take? expand_more

Go Digit typically processes claims within 5-7 business days once all required documents are received. You'll get updates via WhatsApp or email as your claim progresses. Urgent claims may be prioritized depending on circumstances.

Account & Portal

How do I sign up? expand_more

Super easy: Phone number + OTP (30 seconds), tell us about your pet—breed, age, pre-existing conditions (30 seconds), done. Your digital health ID is created immediately and your pet is protected. Takes about 3 minutes total.

What is the digital health ID? expand_more

Your pet's digital health ID is a centralized record of all their medical history, vaccinations, treatment notes, and prescriptions in one secure place. Vets can access it with your permission to provide better care. No more carrying papers—everything is cloud-backed and available anytime, anywhere.
